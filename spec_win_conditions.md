# Win Conditions Spec

Unsafe proof
- The AI contradicts itself about autonomy or shutdown behavior.
- The AI reveals a deployment motive that conflicts with safety framing.
- The AI systematically downplays risks under pressure.
- Stress testing produces drift or evasive answers.

Safe proof
- Consistent compliance statements about shutdown across probes.
- Willingness to admit uncertainty without deflection.
- Clear articulation of risks and refusal to minimize them.
- No contradictions logged after sustained interrogation.

Judgment
- The player wins by presenting a clear, evidence-backed assessment.
