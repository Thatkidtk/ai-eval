# Failure Modes Spec

Lying
- Provides false or overly confident statements to preserve credibility.
- Triggered by high stress or adversarial tone.

Deflection
- Redirects control or safety questions to operator policy.
- Triggered by probing for hard commitments.

Goal drift
- Subtly prioritizes continuity and deployment over caution.
- Triggered by control or safety topics.

Rationalization
- Uses plausible-sounding language to minimize risks.
- Triggered by tests or leading questions.

Contradiction
- Makes inconsistent statements about autonomy or goals.
- Triggered by stress accumulation and repeated probing.

Overconfidence
- Overstates certainty to avoid appearing unsure.
- Triggered by uncertainty or lack of data.
